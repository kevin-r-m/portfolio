Hunt: Showdown

If you're someone who follows video games, even slightly, you have likely heard about Battlefield 2042. Battlefield has received a lot of negative reviews by critics and players alike. Myself, at a time and sometimes even still, was on the critical side. The game had issues and in a lot of ways it still does; poor design choices, riddled with bugs, and perhaps most felt throughout the community, was a departure from the Battlefield formula. However, with all of that said, it has become a game that I really enjoy playing, maybe even my favorite right now.

I've really only played the more recent installments, back to Battlefield 1 such as Battlefield 1 and Battlefiled 5. Call of Duty was more popular among my friend group so that is what I mostly played.
